Delete Your Account

You can delete your Bubbles account directly from within the app at any time. No need to contact us — the option is available in your account settings.

To delete your account in the app:

  • Open the Bubbles app
  • Go to your Profile
  • Tap Settings
  • Select Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your profile and all account information
  • All bubbles you have created
  • Your messages and connections
  • Your location history within the app

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

If you are unable to access the app and need to request account deletion manually, please contact us at privacy@codevival.com with the email address associated with your account.
